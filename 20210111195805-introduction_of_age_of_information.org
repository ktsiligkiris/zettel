#+title: Introduction of Age of Information
#+roam_tags AoI knowledge freshness remote
#+roam_key cite:KoPA17

Age of information (AoI) was introduced in the early 2010s as a notion
to characterize the freshness of the knowledge a system has about a
process observed remotely.

The concept of Age of Information was introduced to quantify the
freshness of the knowledge we have about the status of a remote
system.
