#+title: Teaching to the test

If a school's standardized scores rose while its "non-standardized"
performance moved in the opposite direction, administrators would have
a clear warning sign that "teaching to the test" had set in, and the
pupil's skills were beginning to overfit the mechanics of the test
itself.
