#+title: How to interpret answers

- RTFM and STFW
  There is an ancient hallowed tradition: if you get a reply that
  reads "RTFM", the person who sent it thinks you should have Read the
  Fucking Manual". He or she is almost certainly right. Go and read
  it.
- If you don't understand
  Do not immediately bounce back a demand for clarification.
- Dealing with rudeness
  Much of what looks like rudeness in hacker circles is not intended
  to give offense. Rather it's the product of direct,
  cut-through-the-bullshit communication.
