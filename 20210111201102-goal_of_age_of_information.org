#+title: Goal of Age of Information
#+roam_tags AoI goal estimation prediction vehicular networks
#+roam_key cite:KoPA17

This basic goal opens up a wide range of communication contexts that
span from estimation and prediction, to application such as vehicular
networks and information caching, to name a few.

AoI has spawned relevant performance metrics that are more tractable
such as the Peak Age of Information or the Cost of Update Delay
